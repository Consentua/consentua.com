Consentua is a consent management system from KnowNow Information that helps organisations to achieve data protection compliance, and gives individuals choice and control over how their personal data is used.

Consentua captures users' consent to the use of their personal data. It provides regulatory compliance to organisations that process data, and allows individuals to control how their personal data is being used.
