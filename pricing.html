SaaS

The software as a service (SaaS) comes with the following tiers, with one yearly price:

Standard

£949/yr

Includes:

  • Up to 10,000 user entries
  • Unlimited API calls
  • 10 consent templates & 10 consent services
  • 3 client consent service administrators
  • Online support.
  • 1 x day of consulting to deploy
Contact sales

Pro

£8999/yr

Includes:

  • Up to 100,000 user entries
  • Unlimited API calls
  • Unlimited templates & Unlimited services
  • 10 client consent service administrators
  • Standard support Monday to Friday
  • 2 days of consulting to establish your client consent service
Contact sales

Enterprise (On-Premise / Own Cloud)

This comes in two offerings:

  • Licence to deploy and host Consentua on your own premise servers or cloud service
  • Consentua organised cloud subscription which is billed monthly. Available today on Azure and from next release any Docker compatible cloud provider.
